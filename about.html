About Conjecture.ai

Conjecture.ai is a cutting-edge, privacy-first data analytics engine. Built entirely using modern web technologies, it leverages mathematical heuristics and localized AI logic to automatically unearth hidden trends, anomalies, and correlations in your datasets.

We believe exploring data shouldn't require complex SQL queries, Python scripts, or expensive cloud infrastructure. Just drag, drop, and discover. By running entirely in your browser, your sensitive data NEVER leaves your local machine context except for explicit LLM assistant queries.

Powered by an advanced rule-based mathematical engine, dynamic Chart.js visualizations, and the lightning-fast Groq API for conversational querying, Conjecture.ai acts as your personal data scientist—available instantly, 24/7.
